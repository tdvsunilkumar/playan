That under Article 1723 of the Civil Code of the Philippines, the architect (and engineer) who drew up the plans and specifications for the building/structure is responsible for damages if within fifteen (15) years from the completion of the building/structure, the same should collapse due to defect in the plans or specifications or defects in the ground. 2. That the proposed architectural works shall be in accordance with the architectural plans field with this Office and in conformity with the latest Architectural Code of the Philippines, the National Building Code and its IRR.

3. That Prior to any construction activity, duly accomplished prescribed "Notice of Construction" shall be submitted to the Office of the Building Official.

4. That upon completion of the constrution, the licensed full-time inspector and supervisor/in-charge of construction works shall submit the entry to the logbook duly signed and sealed to the building official including as-built plans and other documents, and shall also accomplish the Certificate of Completion stating that the architectural works conform to the provision of the Architectural Code, the National Building Code and its IRR.
